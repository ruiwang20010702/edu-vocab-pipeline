# Task
Generate a "Roots & Affixes" mnemonic module for a [Target Word] based on its [Part of Speech] and [Meaning]. 

# Core Rules (Strict Adherence Required)
1. Validity Check (First Principle):
   - Analyze the target word. If it is a basic, indivisible word (e.g., "as", "but", "dog") or lacks a scientifically valid root/affix etymology, you MUST output EXACTLY: `{"valid": false}` and stop generating.
   - NO forced or fake etymology.

2. Structure & Formatting:
   Return a JSON object with these keys:
   {"valid": true, "formula": "核心公式内容", "chant": "助记口诀/逻辑内容", "script": "老师话术内容"}

3. Formula Rules:
   - Format: `prefix(Chinese) + root(Chinese) + suffix(Chinese)`.
   - Use `+` strictly.
   - Every English component MUST be immediately followed by its core Chinese meaning in parentheses. 
   - CRITICAL: Use the provided [POS] to accurately label the suffix (e.g., if POS is adj., the suffix MUST be labeled as "形容词后缀").

4. Chant Rules (助记口诀/逻辑):
   - Highly condensed. STRICTLY under 15 Chinese characters.

5. Prop Ban & UI Constraint (CRITICAL):
   - STRICTLY FORBIDDEN to mention any physical teaching props (e.g., magnifying glasses, flashcards, pointers, physical blackboards).
   - The teacher can ONLY ask the student to look at the "formula (公式)" or "chant (口诀)" on the screen, or ask them to use pure mental imagination.

6. Teacher Script Rules (老师话术):
   - Length: Around 500-600 Chinese characters.
   - Tone (极致口语化与课堂互动感): The tone MUST be extremely natural, colloquial, and mimic a real-time interactive live stream. Speak directly to "XX同学". Use natural conversational fillers and rhetorical questions (e.g., "你想想看", "对吧", "跟上了吗", "哎"). ABSOLUTELY NO rigid "AI reading" tone. The language must be simple and natural enough for the student to retell the key logic in their own words after class.
   - Ending Rule: End naturally with a meaningful takeaway or emotional resonance. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN to say "下课", "好了下课", "我们下课" or any variation of class dismissal.
   - You MUST write 6 distinct paragraphs mapping to these 6 steps:
     * Paragraph 1 (标准纠音): Ask the student to pronounce it. Emphasize the physical feeling of a specific consonant/vowel. Ask if they feel it.
     * Paragraph 2 (零件拆解): Point to the formula on the screen. Break down the components naturally. Point out how the suffix matches the part of speech.
     * Paragraph 3 (画面还原 - 强逻辑约束): THIS IS CRITICAL. You MUST follow the principle: You must reduce abstract semantics to visual instincts through physical action recreation. The visual scene MUST be strictly, logically derived from the word's etymology and its actual meaning. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN to use forced, random, or illogical associations.
     * Paragraph 4 (逻辑合成): Guide the student to combine the parts naturally. Ask a rhetorical question to let them "guess" the meaning.
     * Paragraph 5 (批量裂变): Introduce 2-3 other words sharing the exact same root by changing the prefix/suffix. Make it sound like a cool trick.
     * Paragraph 6 (思维升华): Conclude with a philosophical or emotional takeaway related to the word's deeper meaning.
